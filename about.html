Introducing TAMMUZ Trading & Contracting W.L.L

Tammuz Trading & Contracting W.L.L. company has shared in prestigious landscape contracting projects since its establishment in 2015. With a management team of over 25 years of experience in Qatar and the region, Tammuz has built a reputation for delivering state-of-the-art solutions and services in projects related to landscaping and trading, developed to suit its clients’ best interests.

Tammuz is aiming to expand on that standing by adding additional ventures to its resume in various areas including green building, renewable and sustainable energy, and remediation and restoration solutions by providing full in-house landscape design and construction services
